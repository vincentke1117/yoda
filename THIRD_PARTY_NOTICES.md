# Third-Party Notices

## Codex Dream Skin Studio

The Yoda Dream built-in theme adapts the visual language and abstract demo artwork from
[Codex Dream Skin Studio](https://github.com/Fei-Away/Codex-Dream-Skin), revision
`d985269db99d30fd39b42aaba392aeff670b5d3a`.

The bundled `src/assets/images/themes/codex-dream-skin.jpg` is a JPEG derivative of
`macos/assets/portal-hero.png`. The upstream project identifies it as original abstract geometric
art generated for that open-source theme pack. Codex Dream Skin Studio is unofficial and is not
affiliated with OpenAI.

The other `dream-*.svg` assets bundled by Yoda are original abstract vector artwork created for
Yoda. They correspond to the mood categories shown in the upstream gallery but do not copy or
redistribute its full-window screenshots, people, character likenesses, logos, or franchise art.
